Badminton

Badminton is a well known sport and is a fast-paced racket sport with the aim of hitting a shuttlecock over the net and into the opponent's court.
Badminton is a game played by single's or double's on a rectangular court with a high net across the middle.

    Reasons Why Badminton Is A Great Way To Keep You Active!

    • It can help strengthen the heart muscle and limit the risk of blood vessels clogging.
    • The game's fast-paced style offers a great cardiovascular exercise.
    • It improves your reaction time, responsiveness, and coordination, all of which are useful in everyday tasks and in other sports.
    • Badminton is a great way to burn calories because of its high intensity.
